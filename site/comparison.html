04 — Finance & Revenue
Revenue grew 14x since 1999. Volume only grew 4.6x.
Total first-hand sales value for all farmed salmonids (salmon, rainbow trout, trout) climbed from 7.6 billion NOK in 1999 to a peak of 113 billion in 2023. Most of that growth came from price, not volume — the average price per kg rose from 21 NOK in 1999 to 69 NOK in 2023. This is official first-hand price (førstehåndspris), meaning the price processors pay farms, not the consumer retail price. Use the year range controls above to focus on any period.
