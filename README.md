# LaksAtlas

**LaksAtlas** is an interactive transparency dashboard for Norwegian salmon and trout aquaculture. It transforms public fish health and production data into a visual and accessible format for students, researchers, and anyone interested in aquaculture.

The platform combines live Norwegian farm level data with global aquaculture statistics to provide both local insight and long term global context.

---

## What LaksAtlas does

LaksAtlas brings together multiple data sources and presents them through interactive maps, charts, and dashboards.

Users can explore

- Weekly sea lice levels across Norwegian farms  
- Disease occurrence and treatment activity  
- Mortality and production statistics  
- Spatial distribution of aquaculture sites  
- Global aquaculture production across countries, species, and time  

The goal is to make complex datasets readable and useful in a teaching and learning setting.

---

## Educational purpose

LaksAtlas was developed as a digital learning tool for the Fish Physiology course at Nord University.

Students can move beyond static tables and instead work directly with real data. The platform supports classroom discussion on fish welfare, disease dynamics, environmental stress, and production systems.

The global module extends this by allowing comparisons between Norway and major aquaculture producing regions worldwide.

## Data coverage

### Norwegian data
- Coverage from 2012 to present (main report on 2020-2024)  
- Updated weekly  
- Farm level resolution  

### Global data
- Coverage from 1950 to 2024  
- 220 countries  
- More than 4000 species  

---

## Data sources

All data used in LaksAtlas is publicly available.

- **Barentswatch Fishhealth API**  
  Weekly farm level data including sea lice, disease, and treatments  

- **Norwegian Directorate of Fisheries**  
  Official statistics on mortality, production, and escape events  

- **Norwegian Veterinary Institute**  
  Annual fish health reports used for context and interpretation  

- **FAO FishStatJ**  
  Global aquaculture production data across countries, species, and years  

- **OpenStreetMap and Leaflet**  
  Mapping and geographic visualisation  

- **Chart.js**  
  Data visualisation library
